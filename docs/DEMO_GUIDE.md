## ❓ Q&A Preparation

### Expected Questions

#### Technical Questions
**Q: "How accurate is your medical parameter extraction?"**
A: "Our system achieves 95% accuracy on common medical parameters through advanced pattern matching and medical term recognition. We continuously improve accuracy through model training and user feedback."

**Q: "What about patient privacy and HIPAA compliance?"**
A: "We prioritize patient privacy with secure file handling, data encryption, and temporary storage. Our system is designed with HIPAA compliance in mind, and we're implementing additional security measures for production deployment."

**Q: "How do you ensure medical accuracy of AI insights?"**
A: "Our AI insights are generated using carefully engineered prompts with medical disclaimer. We include fallback mechanisms and validate outputs against medical guidelines. The system is designed as a decision-support tool, not a replacement for medical advice."

#### Business Questions
**Q: "How will you compete with existing patient portals?"**
A: "Patient portals focus on data storage and appointment scheduling. MedInsight AI specializes in making medical data understandable and actionable. We complement existing systems rather than replace them."

**Q: "What's your go-to-market strategy?"**
A: "We'll start with direct-to-consumer marketing, partner with diagnostic labs for integration, and expand to healthcare providers. Our freemium model will drive user acquisition."

#### Team Questions
**Q: "What makes your team qualified to build this?"**
A: "Our team combines expertise in AI/ML, full-stack development, and healthcare technology. We've built production-ready systems with modern architectures and have deep understanding of both technical and user experience requirements."

### Difficult Questions

**Q: "How do you handle liability for medical advice?"**
A: "We include clear medical disclaimers and position ourselves as an educational tool. We're consulting with legal experts to ensure compliance and minimize liability. Our terms of service clearly define appropriate use."

**Q: "What happens if your AI gives wrong advice?"**
A: "We implement multiple safety layers: medical disclaimers, conservative recommendations, and always advise consulting healthcare providers. Our risk scoring focuses on flagging potential issues rather than providing definitive diagnoses."
